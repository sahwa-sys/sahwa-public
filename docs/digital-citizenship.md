# Digital Citizenship

## Overview

Digital Citizenship is a core component of the Sahwa ecosystem.

It represents a participant's verified status within the network and provides access to governance rights, ecosystem privileges, and future participation mechanisms.

Digital Citizenship is planned to be represented through NFT-based identity assets on Solana.

---

## Vision

The goal of Digital Citizenship is to create a long-term relationship between ecosystem participants and the Sahwa network.

Rather than acting solely as token holders, users become recognized members of the ecosystem with specific rights and responsibilities.

Digital Citizenship is designed to connect:

- SAH ownership
- HOLD participation
- Governance rights
- Ecosystem privileges
- Reputation systems

---

## Citizenship Model

Digital Citizenship is expected to be represented by a unique NFT.

Each NFT will serve as a proof of ecosystem membership.

The NFT may contain:

- Citizenship level
- Reputation data
- Governance eligibility
- Participation history
- Ecosystem achievements

---

## Eligibility Requirements

Requirements may evolve over time.

Examples include:

- Ownership of SAH tokens
- Active ecosystem participation
- Completion of onboarding requirements
- Minimum HOLD commitments
- Compliance with governance rules

The exact requirements will be defined in future governance proposals.

---

## Citizenship Levels

The ecosystem is expected to support multiple citizenship levels.

Example structure:

| Level | Description |
|---------|---------|
| Citizen | Entry-level participation |
| Contributor | Active ecosystem participant |
| Builder | Long-term contributor |
| Governor | Governance participant |
| Senior Governor | Advanced governance role |

The final level structure may change as the ecosystem develops.

---

## HOLD Integration

Digital Citizenship is closely connected to HOLD mechanics.

Potential requirements may include:

- Minimum frozen balance
- Minimum HOLD duration
- Continuous participation

HOLD demonstrates long-term commitment to the ecosystem and may be required for advanced citizenship levels.

---

## Governance Rights

Digital Citizens are expected to receive governance privileges.

Potential rights include:

- Voting on proposals
- Participating in elections
- Community representation
- Governance discussions
- DAO participation

Governance rights may vary depending on citizenship level.

---

## Ecosystem Privileges

Future benefits may include:

- Access to governance voting
- Access to exclusive ecosystem features
- Participation in special programs
- Early access to ecosystem initiatives
- Community recognition

Additional benefits may be introduced over time.

---

## NFT Architecture

Digital Citizenship NFTs are planned to be implemented on Solana.

The NFT layer may integrate with:

- SAH Token
- HOLD Oracle
- DAO Governance
- Reputation Systems

This architecture enables on-chain verification of citizenship status.

---

## Transferability

The final transferability model has not yet been finalized.

Potential approaches include:

- Transferable NFTs
- Non-transferable Soulbound NFTs
- Hybrid citizenship models

The chosen model will be determined through ecosystem governance and technical evaluation.
